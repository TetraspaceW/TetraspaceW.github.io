Things I Care About

I am a big fan of rationalism and effective altruism. I think at some point this century, the cybersingularity will occur, in which AIs become smarter than people in the non-metaphorical impossible-to-miss way that results in things like planets being dismantled, and that we are currently not on track for this to happen in a way that leaves survivors.

The most effective way for an individual donor to give is through a donor lottery. The Center for Effective Altruism runs one once a year. The most important object-level issue, potentially for disbursing , is AI notkilleveryoneism, and the situation for AI safety is currently looking dire; please read AGI Ruin: A List of Lethalities for a view of the awful state our civilisation is in on this issue.
